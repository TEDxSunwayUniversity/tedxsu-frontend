2019

Uncharted Waves


Ideas act like waves, sparking unexpected impacts. Whether it is a completely new perspective or just a story, they all travel around and have unimaginable effects on humanity. Our event revolves around unconventional ideas and perspectives and we invite you to expand your horizons!

Speakers


Raenuga Indran

Raenuga Indran is extremely ambitious in her approach to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGS) one at a time. She has previously worked on a few programmes such as the Bras Are Not For Bins Campaign and the Social Leadership Programme.

Theophilia Lim Eng Sing

Theophilia Lim Eng Sing, a secretary for the Deaf Media and Technology Association, works as an animator at Joove Enterprise Sdn Bhd and is the founder of a local women's clothing company. Additionally, she offers private mathematics tuition to deaf children

Lu Xanne Boon

As a coach and therapist, Lu Xanne is a member on the Forbes Coaches Council and awarded the Top 100 Global Coaches in the World. She has worked with CEOs, department heads from leading banks, NGO’s, and education institutions. Lu Xanne travels the world to speak, spreading awareness on how we can utilize the power of words to change behaviour.

Zlwin Chew

Zlwin Chew, a full time professional magician and illusionist who has travelled with his shows to over 35 countries around the continents of Europe, America, Middle East, Asia and Australia. He is currently the Royal Caribbean International Cruises’ Resident Magician, onboard the 2nd largest cruise ship in the world, the Quantum of the Seas.

Dr. Yap Kian Meng

Dr. Yap Kian Meng has spent 15 years working in data networks, telecommunications, computer networking industries and electronic control system in machinery. He is currently a member of the Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM).

Dr. Andrew Filmer

Dr. Andrew Filmer has diverse interests, from viola performance to musicology to public speaking. He is a consultant for the Australian and New Zealand Viola Society, and was the first non-American Editor of the Journal of the American Viola Society.

Farah Rani

Farah Rani was trained at the Michael Howard Studios in New York City. She is an avid theatre practitioner who has performed in Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, Tokyo, Adelaide, Brisbane and New York City.

Datuk Yasmin Mahmood

Datuk Yasmin Mahmood, Managing Director of FutureReady Consulting, is widely regarded as Malaysia's global tech icon, and was recognised as one of the world's 100 most influential people in Digital Government by Apolitical, an independent UK-based global network for government.
